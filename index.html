Team Fortress 2

a free-to-play, class-based online PVP game where two teams, RED and BLU, compete in a variety of objectives. Players choose from nine distinct classes (scout, soldier, pyro, demoman, heavy, medic, engineer, sniper, and spy) each with unique abilities, weapons, and roles. Players can also collect, craft, buy, sell, and trade a wide variety of items. The game features classic modes like Capture the Flag and Payload, and includes some training modes to help new players learn the ropes. The game is also 18 years old and still recieves updates from Valve (the company who made it, along with other first of a kind games like Half-Life and Half-Life 2) about every millenia.
